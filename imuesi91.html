Topic: Things We Do For Love

Love is arguably the most talked about verb in the dictionary, in our world today.

Here is a lyric of the song done by 10cc called things we do for love from Genius Website:

"Too many broken hearts have fallen in the river

The lyrics above speaks about how much can be saved if genuine love is shown... examples are as follows:

  3. When we lookout for one another, loneliness would be a thing of the past.
The above examples can be achieved if;
  • We practice what we preach.
  • We Remember we only live once, after which is death and judgement.
  • We live and let live.
